A fun and informative place to chat about moovies and exchange moovie recs.

How Does MoovieTribe Work?

When you register you'll complete the MooviEval- rating each of our selection of 100 moovies. Your ratings on the MooviEval are then compared against those of other MoovieTribe members resulting in a comparative score vs each other member we call Moofinity. We use Moofinity calculations to automatically connect you to other members with Moofinity scores above 90%. This group of like-minded moovie fans becomes your MoovieTribe!

The MoovieTribe Pledge

MoovieTribe is a place for fun and information. We will never "mine" or sell your data...it's just not our thing, period.
